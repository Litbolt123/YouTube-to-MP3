YouTube Downloader Bridge

Connect this extension to the desktop app. Open Settings → Browser extension in YouTube Downloader to copy your token.

The service worker may show as “inactive” in chrome://extensions when idle. That is normal — it wakes automatically when you download or test the connection.

Install once

  1. Keep the YouTube Downloader app running.
  2. In Chrome/Edge: chrome://extensions → Developer mode → Load unpacked.
  3. Select the browser-extension folder from the app (Settings → Open extension folder).
  4. Paste the token above and save.
  5. After updating the extension files, open chrome://extensions and click Reload.
